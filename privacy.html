Privacy Policy

Effective date: 07.07.2026

Suuth is designed to be a quiet daily companion for hydration and gentle self-care.

We try to keep your information simple, private, and close to you.

This Privacy Policy explains what Suuth uses, what stays on your device, and what choices you have.

1. What Suuth is

Suuth helps you track water intake, monitor your daily hydration, and set gentle reminders.

With Suuth Premium, you may also track other drinks.

Suuth is not a medical app. It is not a replacement for advice from a doctor or qualified health professional.

2. Information you may choose to add

You may choose to add or edit a profile name and profile icon inside Suuth.

This is optional.

Suuth also stores app information such as:

  • your water and drink entries
  • your daily hydration goal
  • your reminder settings
  • your selected units
  • your app preferences
  • your premium access status
  • profile details you choose to add, such as age, gender, height, weight, activity level, climate, and goal
  • your selected profile icon and profile color
  • your selected reminder days and reminder times

For the current version of Suuth, this information is stored on your device.

3. Apple Health

Suuth can connect with Apple Health if you give permission.

Apple Health access is optional. Suuth still works if you do not allow Apple Health access.

With your permission, Suuth may read the following Apple Health information:

  • age
  • biological sex
  • height
  • weight

Suuth uses this information to help suggest a more personal daily hydration goal.

With your permission, Suuth may write water intake to Apple Health.

If you track other drinks in Suuth, Suuth may calculate a water-equivalent hydration amount for those drinks. With your permission, Suuth may write that water-equivalent hydration amount to Apple Health as water intake.

For example, a drink may count differently toward your hydration total depending on its hydration value inside Suuth.

You can change Apple Health permissions at any time in the Health app or iOS Settings.

Suuth does not sell Apple Health data. Suuth does not use Apple Health data for advertising. Suuth does not use Apple Health data to track you across other apps or websites.

4. Notifications

Suuth may send gentle water reminders if you turn reminders on.

These are local notifications.

This means the reminders are scheduled on your device. They are not sent from a Suuth server.

You can change or turn off notifications at any time in Suuth settings or iOS Settings.

5. Purchases and subscriptions

Suuth Premium is handled through Apple’s App Store purchase system.

Suuth does not collect or store your payment card details.

Apple may process information related to your purchase, subscription, renewal, cancellation, or refund in accordance with Apple’s terms and privacy practices.

6. Analytics

The current version of Suuth does not use third-party analytics tools.

Suuth does not currently use Mixpanel, Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar analytics services.

We may add privacy-respecting analytics in a future version to understand basic app usage and improve Suuth.

If analytics are added later, this Privacy Policy will be updated first.

Suuth will not use analytics to collect Apple Health data, age, biological sex, gender, height, weight, hydration amounts, drink entries, profile names, or other sensitive personal health details.

7. Ads and tracking

Suuth does not show ads.

Suuth does not track you across other apps or websites.

8. Cloud sync

The current version of Suuth does not sync your hydration data through Suuth cloud services.

We may add optional cloud sync in a future version to support cross-device use.

If cloud sync is added later, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that feature is released.

9. Data deletion

Because Suuth stores your app data on your device in the current version, you can remove local Suuth data by deleting the app from your device.

You may also delete individual water or drink entries inside the app where this feature is available.

You may delete your hydration history from Suuth in Settings. If Apple Health is connected, Suuth will also try to remove matching hydration entries it added to Apple Health.

10. Children’s privacy

Suuth is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Suuth grows.

If we make meaningful changes, we will update the effective date and make the new version available.
